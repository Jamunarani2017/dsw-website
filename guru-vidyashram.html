Guru Vidyashram

Founded in the year 2012, Guru Vidhyasharam at Chennai focuses on the education and therapy services for children with special needs. Our school lays great emphasis on skill development and academic learning appropriately tailored to the student's potential. We enable the parents of our enrolled students to work with us in tandem to make learning seamless. Our objective is to prepare the child to the extent possible for getting into the mainstream education system.

Our School offers:

Early Intervention Program
  • Pre-Primary
  • Primary
Skills Training Unit
  • Pre- Vocational
  • Vocational Training
National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) Exam
Integrated Treatment Program
  • Physiotherapy
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Speech-Language Pathology
  • Sensory Integration
  • Neuro Development
  • Therapy
  • Clinical Psychology Services
  • Counseling
  • Assessment

Guru Vidhyashram is registered under the Govt. of Tamil Nadu and Commissionerate for the Welfare of the Differently Abled Society.  Registration No: 424/2020
