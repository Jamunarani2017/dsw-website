Welcome to DSW Trust

Driven by a shared passion for serving our society and an extraordinary commitment to purpose, Dhanabakkiammal Social Welfare (DSW)Trust was established under the Govt of Tamil Nadu Societies in the year —–

Mr. Ganesh Latchumanan, an NCCA accredited Rehabilitation Trainer, and Mrs. Saranya Ganesh, a well-respected Neuro-Developmental Therapy specialist, have been deeply involved in addressing the need for special education and social empowerment of children for more than a decade. Their enthusiasm and a clear sense of purpose have inspired many individuals associated with them in coming together to improve the quality of life for children with special needs from not so fortunate backgrounds.

The DSW Trust operates schools for physically, mentally, and or visually challenged children with individualized intervention therapy towards making them self-reliant to the extent reasonably possible. The Trust does not limit its endeavors to classroom training alone. It provides an opportunity to work in a related field to survive life with minimal or no external support. DSW Trust collaborates with the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) in providing an opportunity for those students who enroll late into an education system. It lays equal emphasis on education and skill development.

The Trust firmly believes that this contemporary social work model can add value to society and various public health initiatives undertaken by Govt. agencies..

Who we are

DSW Trust:

DHANABAKKIAMMAL SOCIAL WELFARE TRUST is a humble initiative of Mr. Ganesh latchumanan and Mrs. Saranya Ganesh, both from a very simple ordinary family who always cherished a better dream, not only for themselves but for the immediate surroundings. Who dream to share happiness around and give to others whatever best they can in their reach, with a team of young like-minded association, they formed DSW Trust (DHANABAKKIAMMAL SOCIAL WELFARE TRUST) registered under Govt of Tamilnadu Societies.

DSW Trust, Chennai prime objective has been to make Physically, Mentally, visually impaired persons both self-reliant and contributing members of the society.

we trained them in special way and we create a special style to those people. We did not stop our service by giving the training itself we provide the opportunity to work in that field what they trained, so we opened a new working environment for those people to survive life without any other support.

 

DSW Trust, Chennai has also initiating services for children with visual impairment and additional disabilities since 2012. School for Multiple disabilities provides individualized intervention to multisensory impaired/deaf-blind children.

The organization lays special emphasis on education and skill development. DSW Trust, Chennai aims to send each and every Special NEED child into mainstream school. It highly advocates the concept of integrated education for social fulfilment. It also dreams to embed computer education and assistive technology into the lives of every visually impaired bringing them closer to the world. We ardently focus on qualitative education and skill development.

To achieve education for all disabled children, we are in collaboration with National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) to provide opportunity for those students who come late to the education system.
